Pizza Dough Recipe

Description:

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Start by mixing flour and yeast in a bowl
  2. Add a teaspoon of sugar dissolved in a little bit of water
  3. Add a pinch of salt
  4. Knead the dough with your hands, or using a food processor
  5. Let the Dough Come to Room Temperature
  6. Prepare Your Surface With Oil
  7. Shape the Dough
  8. Decorate it, bake it, and enjoy!
